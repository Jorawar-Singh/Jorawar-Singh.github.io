Translating Textbooks

IISER Mohali has embarked upon a mission to translate outstanding textbooks that are in English to local languages so that students who are in rural areas and are not familiar with English have access to high-quality learning material. As part of this effort, the first translation of a classic Physics textbook into Hindi is almost complete. Many such translations are underway.
