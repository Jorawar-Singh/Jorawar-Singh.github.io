Generating Education Material

This is oriented towards teaching in the school and college levels. IISER Mohali faculty members and students have come up with several teaching aids in terms of new experiments that can be performed in class, written material, instruments and demonstration material. These can be used to make classroom teaching much more meaningful. These are provided to teachers free of cost.
